The rich history of Sirmour

Sirmour, a region in Himachal Pradesh with deep historical roots, was a
significant princely state established by Raja Rasalu around the 11th century,
evolving from ancient Kulinda tribes, with its name linked to dominance or Sirmouri
Taal, and saw rule by Rajputs, Gurkhas, and later British influence,leaving behind forts,
temples,and tales of cultural clashes and royal lineage, notably connected to the Sikh
Gurus and the Jaipur
Royals.

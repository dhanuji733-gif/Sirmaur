Popular Destinations

HARIPURDHAR

Haripurdhar is a scenic high-altitude town in Himachal Pradesh known for Maa Bhangayani Temple and Churdhar Peak treks. It offers stunning Himalayan views, pleasant summers, snowy winters, and rich cultural heritage.

RENUKA

Renuka is a revered Hindu goddess, considered an incarnation of Goddess Parvati. She represents purity, devotion, and maternal strength and holds deep spiritual importance in Himachal Pradesh.

SHILLAI

Government College Shillai offers quality education in Humanities, Commerce, Science and postgraduate courses. It promotes academic excellence and character development in a beautiful Himalayan setting.
